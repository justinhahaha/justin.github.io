Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 800 g chicken
  • 1/2-1 coconut milk
  • 180 g curry paste
  • 3 large potato
  • 3 large onion
  • 3 stalks curry leaves

Instruction

  1. First, saute curry paste in hot oil until fragrant. Add in chicken. Continue to saute until chicken releases oil.
  2. Second, add in water, tamarind paste, curry leaves, potato, onion, sugar and salt. Let chicken simmer in slow fire until potatoes becomes soft and chicken is cooked thoroughly.
  3. Lastly, add in coconut milk and cook until oil starts to separate. Remove and serve.
